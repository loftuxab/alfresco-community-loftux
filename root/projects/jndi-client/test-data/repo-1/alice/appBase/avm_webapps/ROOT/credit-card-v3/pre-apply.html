FEATURES: SUMMARY: 
Typical APR (Annual Percentage Rate)Typical rate 15.9% APR (variable) - that's 1.2408% a month.
Promotional rateProvided you operate your account within the terms and conditions:
0% p.a. - that's  0.0000% a month on card purchases for three months from account opening, and 0% p.a.  - that's 0.0000% a month on balance transfers and credit card cheques for twelve months from account opening. 
Other rates 15.9% p.a. (variable) on card purchases, balance transfers and credit card cheques - that's 1.2408% a month. 
24.9% p.a. (variable) on cash transactions (including money transfers) - that's 1.8735% a month.
Interest free periodUp to 56 days on card purchases as long as you pay your statement balance in full and on time every month, otherwise interest will be charged as set out below.
Interest charging informationCard purchases, balance transfers, credit card cheques and cash transactions (including money transfers): we will charge interest on all transactions, fees, other charges and interest, starting on the transaction date and ending on the date you pay the bill in full. We work out interest each day so the earlier you make payment, the less interest you will have to pay.
How your balance is 
paid off		Items at lower rates of interest on your statement will be paid before items at higher rates of interest.
Monthly minimum paymentThe lesser of:
  • 2.25% of your balance (min. £5) or
  • charges for Payment Protection Cover plus interest and fees, plus £5 or
  • your balance, if it's under £5.                                    
Credit limitWe will choose your credit limit and tell you what it is. We may vary it at any time and write to let you know. The maximum credit limit that you can have under this agreement is £50,000.
Annual fee£0
Other charges2% handling fee (min. £3) for credit card cheques and balance transfers. 
3% handling fee (min. £3) for cash transactions(including money transfers). 
2.75% commission on foreign currency transactions.
Default charges£12 for going over your credit limit, paying late, or payments that bounce.
